About Me

Background

I went from having a good, happy life to eventually turning to a life of drugs and sadness.

Prison Life

Quickly after about a year of heavy drug using, I started to spiral even further and eventually ended up in prison, not once but twice. After getting out of prison the second time, I decided to start my life over and use my pain and past experiences to shape my future.

Healthy Lifestyle/Art Projects

I eventually turned my life around to be focused on working out and creating art. Now I am looking to give back to those that are struggling like I was. I would like to share my story of hope and the idea that recovery is possible through determination and inner strength.
